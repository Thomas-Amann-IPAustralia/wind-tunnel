⚠️ Demonstration Prototype — Limitations & Scope

This is an interactive user interface mockup designed to illustrate the proposed ATO Deduction Assistant concept. Please note the following boundaries of this demonstration:

  • No Real Tax Advice: All guidance, calculations, and policy interpretations shown are simulated and must not be used for actual tax preparation.
  • No Live Data Integration: This prototype is entirely self-contained. It does not connect to real taxpayer accounts, active ATO databases, or live case management systems.
  • Simulated AI & RAG: There is no active Large Language Model (LLM) or Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) backend. Responses are pre-scripted to demonstrate how the "creativity dial" and source citations would function.
  • Static Policy References: Cited tax rulings (e.g., TR 2020/1) are illustrative placeholders and do not reflect real-time policy updates.
