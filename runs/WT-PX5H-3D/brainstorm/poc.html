APS Tech Navigator — Interactive Proof of Concept Limitations

This is an illustrative interface demonstration. It runs entirely in your browser and does not connect to any real systems or databases. Specifically:

  • No Real AI Processing: Chat responses and generated briefs are pre-scripted simulations, not live LLM outputs.
  • No Real Data Access: The system does not query actual APS software catalogs, internal policy documents, or OFFICIAL databases.
  • Simulated Voice Input: The microphone button simulates speech-to-text using pre-defined scenario scripts.
  • No Legal or Procurement Advice: Recommendations are purely illustrative and do not constitute formal procurement approval or legal compliance checks.
