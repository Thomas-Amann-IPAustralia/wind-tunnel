<!-- section: problem -->
## 1. Problem

Public servants in the APS often lack the technical literacy or awareness of available tools to effectively identify and solve operational problems, leading to a gap between identifying a need and knowing how to address it with technology.

<!-- section: solution -->
## 2. Proposed solution

A chatbot interface that acts as a technical advisor, guiding APS staff through their specific problems and suggesting appropriate technology solutions or pathways based on internal OFFICIAL information.

<!-- section: users_stakeholders -->
## 3. Users and stakeholders

The primary users are APS staff across various roles who encounter operational hurdles but lack the technical expertise to identify solutions. Stakeholders include IT and procurement departments, who must manage the downstream implementation of suggested solutions, and the broader public, who benefit from a more efficient and capable public service.

<!-- section: data -->
## 4. Data

The system will ingest and reference internal documentation classified up to OFFICIAL, alongside publicly available information. This includes internal policy documents, existing software catalogs, and potentially past project documentation to ensure recommendations are grounded in approved organizational knowledge.
