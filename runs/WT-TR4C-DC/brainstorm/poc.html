Proof of Concept Limitations & Disclaimers

This is an interactive user interface demonstration designed for evaluation purposes. It simulates the user experience of the proposed Canberra Business Navigator.

  • No Real AI or LLM: There is no active Large Language Model processing queries. All conversational responses are pre-authored mock scenarios.
  • No Live Data Integration: The system does not query real ACT Government legislation, Access Canberra databases, or live funding guidelines.
  • No Data Storage: No information entered into the chat inputs is saved, transmitted, or logged for audit purposes.
  • Informational Only: The content shown is illustrative and does not constitute official legal, regulatory, or business advice.
