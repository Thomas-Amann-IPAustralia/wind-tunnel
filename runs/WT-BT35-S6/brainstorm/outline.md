<!-- section: problem -->
## 1. Problem

Managers often struggle to provide consistent, objective, and timely performance feedback to their staff, leading to gaps in development and potential bias in evaluations.

<!-- section: solution -->
## 2. Proposed solution

An AI-powered assessment tool that analyzes performance data and feedback logs to generate draft performance summaries and development suggestions for managers to review.

<!-- section: users_stakeholders -->
## 3. Users and stakeholders

Primary users are managers; staff members are the primary stakeholders affected by the resulting assessments.

<!-- section: data -->
## 4. Data

The system will likely process internal performance review documents, goal-tracking data, and potentially anonymized meeting transcripts or feedback logs.
